I visited this cafe with a group of friends, the staff were so friendly and helped us choose the perfect game for us.
The food was delicious and so many tasty treats for those with a sweet tooth

This café is the definition of cosy, a perfect place for rainy days!
The staff have a wide range of board games to choose from, even some that are dedicated to solo play while you enjoy their superb coffee.

The Short Rest café is a great hub for newcomers to board games and other table top games.
I still remember the first time I visited on a more quiet day and a member of staff took the time to sit and show me how to play one of their favourite games.

This café really makes you feel like your at home, from the cosy furniture to the friendly smiles as you walk in the door, picking a game from the shelf gives me the same sense of wonder I experience as a kid on those rainy board game filled days. I could spend the whole day here!
