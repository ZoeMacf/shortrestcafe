About Us

The Short Rest Café is a new local hub for the board game and table top gaming community, we provide a cosy space for groups of people to relax and play games together alongside our tasty treats and fabulous coffee!

There are also a select amount of board games and accessories which can be purchased by our customers, more details on what we have available can be located in the cafe.

We also host a weekly games night with many prizes to be won, for newcomers and veterans alike!
